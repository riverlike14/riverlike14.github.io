# Kelly Criterion

## Formula
> $A \cdot (2p - 1)$

### Example
Donghyeon plays a game and he bets $x$ amount of money. If he wins he receives $2x$ but he gets nothing if he loses. He wins the game with probability $70\%$ and he can play the game infinitely many times. How much does he has to bet in order to make his money increases as fast as possible?
